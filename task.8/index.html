Character Details

Kaedehara Kazuha

Boss of All Boss

General

Kaedehara Kazuha is a playable Anemo character in Genshin Impact.

A wandering samurai of the once-famed Kaedehara Clan with an ability to read the sounds of nature, Kazuha is a temporary crewmember of The Crux. Despite being burdened by the many happenings of his past, he still maintains an easygoing disposition.

Gameplay

Normal Attack
Performs up to 5 rapid strikes.

Charged Attack
Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to unleash 2 rapid sword strikes.

Plunging Attack: Midare Ranzan
DMG is converted to Anemo DMG and creates a small wind tunnel.

Constellations

Constellation Level 6, Crimson Momiji: "After using Chihayaburu or Kazuha Slash, Kaedehara Kazuha gains an Anemo Infusion for 5s. Additionally, each point of Elemental Mastery will increase the DMG dealt by Kaedehara Kazuha's Normal, Charged, and Plunging Attacks by 0.2%."
